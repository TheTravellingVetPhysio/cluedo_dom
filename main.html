Rules

You have 50 turns to solve the mystery.

In each turn you can choose to:

  • Move to a (new) room by clicking the room you want to move to.
  • Research the room you are in and either a suspect or an item by clicking the relevant magnifying glass.
  • Make your final guess of who, where and how by clicking the question marks.

You only have one final guess!

If your guess is wrong you are out (and if you are the last detective the game ends and the culprit goes free)!

If your guess is correct you win and the culprit is caught.

If you want to reset the game you can at all times press the reset button.
